About Helldivers 2

Helldivers 2 is a third-person shooter where you fight for freedom, liberty, and managed democracy as an elite soldier of Super Earth.

The galaxy is under siege from alien invaders, and only the Helldivers can save humanity. Equipped with the finest weapons and stratagems that Super Earth has to offer.

Experience intense cooperative gameplay with up to 4 players. Use teamwork, strategy, and superior firepower to complete missions and extract safely. Friendly fire is always on - watch your shots!

The year is 2184. Super Earth has achieved peace through strength and unity. Now, alien threats emerge from the galaxy's darkest corners, seeking to destroy our perfect society. As a Helldiver, you are humanity's last hope.

Dynamic galactic war, procedurally generated missions, massive arsenal of weapons and stratagems, destructible environments, and intense combat scenarios await every Helldiver.

Helldiver Training Manual

Welcome, Cadet! Your journey to becoming an elite Helldiver begins here. Learn the fundamentals of combat, teamwork, and democratic values. Master your primary weapon, understand friendly fire protocols, and always remember: the only good bug is a dead bug!

Stratagems are your lifeline on the battlefield. From orbital bombardments to supply drops, master the input sequences to call down Super Earth's finest support. Practice makes perfect - and perfect execution saves lives!

Elite Helldivers understand terrain advantage, enemy weaknesses, and team coordination. Learn to adapt your loadout for different mission types, master the art of strategic retreats, and become the tip of Super Earth's spear!
